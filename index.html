❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I check if I am eligible for a scheme?›
Each scheme has a defined eligibility criteria including age, income, occupation, caste, and state. On our platform, you can see the eligibility section for each scheme. You can also use the personalized recommendations feature after creating a profile.
Are all schemes listed here from official government sources?›
Yes. All schemes on Yojana Setu are sourced from official government websites ending in .gov.in. We clearly show the official link and last-updated date for each scheme. We do not list any unofficial schemes.
Is this service free? Do I need to pay to apply?›
Yojana Setu is completely free to use. Government schemes are also free to apply for. Never pay anyone to apply on your behalf — that is illegal. CSC centers may charge a nominal service fee (usually ₹25-50) for helping with applications.
How do I get notified about new schemes?›
Register for a free account or subscribe with just your email to receive notifications. You will get alerts when new schemes are launched, applications open, or deadlines are near. You can also set category preferences to get only relevant alerts.
I don't have internet. How can I access schemes?›
Visit your nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or Gram Panchayat. You can also call government helplines like 14555 (Ayushman Bharat) or 155261 (PM Kisan). Many schemes can also be applied through local bank branches.
How do I track my application status?›
Once you apply, note down your application/registration number. Most schemes have a dedicated status tracking page on their official websites. You can also visit your nearest CSC to check status. Application reminders are available in your Yojana Setu dashboard.
